# Pricing PRD — Vastora CRM

**Version:** 1.0.0-draft

---

## 1. Pricing Philosophy

- **Transparent** — no hidden per-contact fees (unlike HubSpot)
- **Per-user** — predictable scaling
- **Module-tiered** — pay for what you use
- **India-first** — INR pricing, GST-compliant invoicing

---

## 2. Plans

### Starter — ₹999/user/month (₹799 annual)

**Includes:** HRM, Messenger, Employee Portal, Basic Dashboard  
**Ideal for:** 10–50 employee companies needing HR digitization first

### Growth — ₹1,499/user/month (₹1,199 annual)

**Includes:** Everything in Starter + CRM + Projects  
**Ideal for:** Agencies, IT services, professional services with sales pipeline

### Enterprise — from ₹1,999/user/month (custom)

**Includes:** Everything + Finance + Support + AI + SSO  
**Ideal for:** 200+ employees, multi-department, compliance needs

---

## 3. Annual Discount

20% off when billed annually. Displayed as monthly equivalent on pricing page.

---

## 4. Free Trial

- **14 days** on Growth tier (all CRM features)
- No credit card required
- Max 10 users during trial
- Auto-downgrade to read-only after trial + 3-day grace

---

## 6. Billing Rules

- GST 18% added for Indian customers
- Pro-rata upgrade; downgrade at cycle end
- Failed payment: 3 retries over 7 days → suspend
- Refund: pro-rata within 14 days of first purchase only

---

| Competitor | Comparable Plan | Price/user |

| Vastora Growth | CRM + HRM + Projects | ₹1,499 |
