## Article Template

Each KB article follows:

1. **Title** — Action-oriented ("How to convert a lead to a deal")
2. **Summary** — 1 sentence
3. **Prerequisites** — Role, plan tier, module enabled
4. **Steps** — Numbered with screenshots
5. **Related articles** — 2–3 links
6. **Last updated** — Date
